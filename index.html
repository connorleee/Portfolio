About Me

I have been in the construction industry for 3 years as a Heavy Civil construction contractor working on the management side of projects. Although the construction industry is an old industry, I've had the opportunity to develop a drone program with my company to try and utilize up and coming technology. Drones can use GPS and photos to generate high resolution images as well as create 3D models of job sites for quantity tracking purposes to use as backup for payment.

I am now looking to learn an entirely new skill and jump into a more technology driven indutry that promotes many more fast paced innovations in a similar manner as drones in construction.

  • Amtrak - King Street Station Improvements
